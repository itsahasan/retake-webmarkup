Is a nicely built, fast performing, well-equipped laptop for way under $1,000 too much to ask for? The Lenovo IdeaPad 3 14 firmly answers, offering that and more for around just $520. For that low price, it delivers snappy speeds, USB-C ports, a comfortable keyboard, and even a fingerprint scanner, all in a compact body with good battery life.
